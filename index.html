Dumble shaped interlocking paving stones

Dumbbell-shaped (or 'I-shape') interlocking paving stones are heavy-duty, concrete blocks designed with a narrow center and wider ends. This unique shape locks tightly together in all directions, making them highly resistant to shifting and ideal for areas with heavy vehicular traffic like driveways, industrial parks, and commercial compounds.
